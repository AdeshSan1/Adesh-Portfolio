Fast forward a couple years, where I gained experience in programming and robotics, I decided I was ready to give it my all.

I was able to create a circuit using a multiplexing technique to lower the number of Input and Outpin (I/O) pins required to power several LEDs. The cube used 27 LEDs (9 red, 9 blue, and 9 white) with 9 (3 X 3) LEDs per layer. Each layer was connected to a transistor, which emitted to the ground when energized with 5V. Furthermore, all vertical LEDs (3 in a column) were connected using a common cathode. Somehow, I managed to get this right on my first try with the circuit diagram shown.
